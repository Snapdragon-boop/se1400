Keeping your flute clean.

cealning your flute is one of the first things to learn.

Cleaning your flute is important because it keeps the inside dry and prevents moisture from building up, which can damage the pads over time. Wiping it out after you play also helps your flute stay in good condition so it sounds its best every time you use it. Taking a minute to clean it might seem small, but it actually helps your flute last longer and play better.

How to put away your flute.

Putting away your flute the right way helps keep it safe and in good condition. After you’re done playing, you should gently take it apart piece by piece, wipe out any moisture, and place each part in its correct spot in the case so nothing gets bent or scratched. Closing the case securely and storing it somewhere safe makes sure your flute stays clean, protected, and ready for your next practice.
