About Ruth

Ruth.

Ruth is an amazing flute teacher because she makes learning feel comfortable and fun while still helping you improve. She has a great sense of humor, but she also pays attention to the things you struggle with and helps you fix them in a way that actually makes sense. One of her favorite sayings is “practice makes permanent,” which basically means the more you repeat something the right way, the more natural it becomes. She’s the kind of teacher who supports you, pushes you, and makes you feel confident about getting better every week.
